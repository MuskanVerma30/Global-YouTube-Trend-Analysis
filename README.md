**Global YouTube Statistics Analysis**



**Project Overview**

This project analyzes global YouTube channel statistics to identify factors influencing:

\- Channel growth

\- Audience engagement

\- Revenue generation



**Tools \& Technologies**

\- Python

\- Pandas

\- NumPy

\- Matplotlib

\- Seaborn



**Key Insights**

\- Entertainment and Music channels dominate engagement.

\- Subscriber growth strongly impacts monetization.

\- United States and India lead the creator ecosystem.

\- Audience reach matters more than upload frequency.



**Dataset**

Global YouTube Statistics Dataset (1006 channels, 28 features)



**Visualizations**

\- Correlation Heatmap

\- Subscriber Dominance Analysis

\- Country-wise Analysis

\- Channel Creation Trends

\- Recent Viewership Analysis
